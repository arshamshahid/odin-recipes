Tomato Rice

Maldivian Tomato Rice

A flavor rich rice made with tomato paste and spices and smoked tuna.

Ingredients

Rice, Tomato paste, Curry leaves, Maldivian chillie, Onion, Smoked tuna, Garlic, Salt, Chillie sauce, Cooking oil.

Steps 1

Start by cutting the onions, chillie, smoked tuna and garlic in to small cubes.

Step 2

Add cut onions toa pan and add 2 table spoons of cooking oil. Set the stove heat to low and keep stiring for 5 mins. Now add the garlic and chillie and continue to stir at low heat until the onion becomes golden brown.

Add 2 table spoons of salt and the smoked tuna and keep stiring for 5 mins. Now add 3 cans of tomato paste mix well and close the lid and leave it for 5 mins. Once done add chillie sauce to the pan and mix well.

Step 3

Bring the finished sauce and add rice in to it and stir well to mix it up.
